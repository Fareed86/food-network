About Me

A native of Buxton, Maine, Danielle is a small town girl with a big city attitude. Classically trained in baking and pastry from Johnson & Wales University, Danielle has shared her talents in restaurants throughout Vacationland, from exciting, fast-paced Portland to the sleepy shores of Vinalhaven, an island off the coast of Rockland. Her work as a pastry chef has been highlighted in Down East magazine and the Portland Press Herald. Danielle loves to share her knowledge and experiences through great storytelling.
